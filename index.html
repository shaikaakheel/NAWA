A Note From The Founder

"Why should something we have grown up wearing ever feel outdated?"

Like many women, I grew up wearing salwar kameez — it has always meant more than clothing to me. It represents comfort, culture, and familiarity. As I grew older, I realised many of us still love it, but want to feel elevated, not outdated or forced.

That thought became Nawa — a carefully curated collection where every piece is personally selected for quality and comfort, staying true to our roots while feeling stylish and minimal.

From pure cotton hand-embroidery suits to Kota cotton articles, we bring pieces that are both trending and timeless — most as unique single pieces, with Kota Cotton offered across multiple colourways so more women can enjoy it.
